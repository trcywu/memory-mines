Memory Mines

Instructions

  • Mines will appear in random tiles at the beginning of the game
  • Memorize the position of the mines
  • Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to direct the path from Start to Finish without hitting any of the mines
  • The dot will only move one square up, down, left or right
  • The number of mines will increase after each play so that the games gets harder
  • The game ends when you hit a mine and the final score will be revealed
